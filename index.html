Privacy Policy

At Grocyray, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and shipping address when you make a purchase or create an account on our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used to process orders, manage your account, and provide you with updates on our products and services.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with third-party service providers to facilitate payments, shipping, and marketing. We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and personalize content. You can adjust your cookie settings in your browser at any time.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal information. Contact us at privacy@grocyray.com for any inquiries.

Shipping Policy

We offer reliable shipping services to ensure your groceries arrive fresh and on time.

1. Shipping Methods

Choose from Standard or Express shipping options at checkout.

2. Shipping Costs

Standard shipping is free for orders over ₹50. For orders below this amount, a flat rate of ₹5 applies. Express shipping is available for an additional ₹10.

3. Delivery Times

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days, while Express shipping ensures delivery within 1-2 business days.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email to monitor your delivery status.

5. International Shipping

We currently ship within the India only. International shipping options will be available soon.
